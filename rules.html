PPFL Rule Book

Rules

4 quarters – 15 minute running clock

Full tackle unless rainy

No rushing QB unless they don’t pass or run after 10 seconds

QB is fair game if they run the ball

Touchdowns count as 7 (no extra point)

After a touchdown punt to the other team at half field

QB cannot run until after 10 seconds

QB can only hand it off on 4th down

5 minute warm up before games
